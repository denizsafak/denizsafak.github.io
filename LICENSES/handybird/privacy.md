# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** May 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how HandyBird ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application. 

## 1. Our Privacy Philosophy: Local-First
Our core mission is to provide media tools that respect user privacy. Unlike cloud-based converters, HandyBird processes your videos, images, and documents locally on your device. Your personal files never leave your device for the purpose of conversion.

## 2. Information We Collect
Because we do not use a registration system, we do not collect your name, email address, or phone number. However, we collect certain technical data:
* **Usage Data:** Information about how you interact with the App (e.g., features used, conversion types).
* **Device Identifiers:** We may collect anonymized IDs (such as IDFA for iOS or Advertising ID for Android) to facilitate advertising and analytics.
* **Crash Reports:** Technical logs generated if the App crashes, used solely for debugging.

## 3. Google Ads and Third-Party SDKs
The App integrates Google AdMob to display advertisements. 
* **Data Sharing:** Google may use device identifiers and cookies to serve personalized ads based on your interests. 
* **Opt-Out:** You can opt-out of personalized advertising through your device settings ("Limit Ad Tracking" on iOS or "Opt out of Ads Personalization" on Android).
* **Third-Party Policies:** We encourage you to review Google’s Privacy Policy regarding how they manage ad data.

## 4. Permissions
The App requires the following permissions to function:
* **Media Library/Storage:** To allow you to select files for conversion and save the output. We utilize the modern "Photo Picker" API (Android 14+ / iOS 17+) to ensure we only access the specific files you authorize.
* **Internet Access:** Required for loading advertisements and sending anonymized crash reports.

## 5. Data Retention
Since we do not store your files on our servers, we have no "user data" to delete regarding your media. Any anonymized analytics or crash data is retained for a maximum of 14 months to help us improve the App’s performance.

## 6. Children's Privacy
HandyBird does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe we have inadvertently collected such data via our third-party ad partners, please contact us.

## 7. International Compliance (GDPR / KVKK)
* **For Users in the EU (GDPR):** We act as a Data Controller for technical identifiers. You have the right to access, rectify, or erase your data.
* **For Users in Türkiye (KVKK):** We process technical data in compliance with the Law on the Protection of Personal Data. 
Since our App is "Privacy-by-Design" and processes files locally, we fulfill the highest standards of data protection.

## 8. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the App. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
